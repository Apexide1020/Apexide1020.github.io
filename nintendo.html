No. 5: Minecraft

Cost: £19.99

Minecraft is a great game for console especially when you can play on the go. Since it has single player co-op and online multiplayer, it makes the list with its high customisation, compatibility and functionality. It's a perfect game for anyone that wants complete freedom to build, create and fight.

No. 4: Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Cost: £59.99

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is one of the best games to play with friend, family, or even a low to high level NPC it is one of the best sidescroll fighting games. It also includes a thrilling story including four different difficulties.

No. 3: Splatoon 3

Cost: £49.99

Splatoon is one of Nintendo's most beloved game franchises to be made. It is an online shooter game in which you pertisapate in teams of four and has a campaign with interesting lore. There's even festivals in which you choose one of three teams and at a certain point you can face off all of the three teams, one team of four, and, two teams of two, all of which are aiming to earn the highest amount of points. it is an amazing game for people that love competitive shooters and enjoy short term matches.

No. 2: Super Mario Odyssey

Cost: £49.99

Super Mario Odyssey was created in 2017 and still is an amazing game to this day. it is an open world adventure game with an objective of saving Princess Peach alongside an ally named Cappy. Use your newfound abilitys to save the princess and restore peace to the world.

No. 1: The Legend Of Zelda: Breath of the wild/ Tears Of The Kingdom.

Cost(Both): £59.99

These games have been paired in number one because of certain preferences between the two games. Both have an amazing story and both follow lore of every other zelda game. Your objective in the game is to save Zelda and Hyrule along with killing Ganon. You need to utilize every skill that you have in game. It is a fully offline game and is perfect for people that love action and strategy games with a good story.
